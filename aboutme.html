About Me

I'm a 23 year old Portuguese guy, certified in Computer Systems Management and Programming (European Qualifications Framework Level 4), who aspires to be an indie game developer. Since graduating highschool, I have been refining my existing skills and constantly looking for new knowledge.
My goal is to create games that people can enjoy, without having to worry about microtransactions or "pay to win" elements.
Fun gameplay, captivating worlds, solid level design, memorable characters, an entertaining story and a good balance of challenge/reward are my focus.
