About Me

My name is Carlos, I am a competitive programming enthusiast who loves to stream while coding.
Ever since I was a teenager, I have been fascinated with computers and programming.
Recently, I discovered the world of competitive programming and fell in love with it.
The thrill of solving complex problems and the satisfaction of finally cracking a tough challenge kept me hooked!
On my Twitch channel, I share my passion for competitive programming with my viewers while I tackle various problems from online coding platforms like CodeChef, LeetCode, etc...
My channel is a friendly and supportive community where everyone is welcome to learn and grow together. Whether you are a seasoned programmer or a complete beginner, there's something for everyone on my channel.
